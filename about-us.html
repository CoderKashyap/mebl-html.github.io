Our story

Brewing Greatness, One Summit at a Time

Our brewery in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, stands as a testament to our dedication, with an installed capacity of 10 lakh hectolitres (1.5 million cases per month) and one of the finest canning lines in the world.

Setting up this brewery in just nine months was our first "summit" moment—proof that we're as restless as a bubbling brew when it comes to achieving greatness.

Our Story

"From our first brew to iconic brands, MEBL's journey is a story of passion, innovation, and excellence."

1999–08

Commercial Operations & Collaboration

The foundation for the cutting-edge Simrol plant was laid, marking a milestone in advancing production capabilities. Additionally, collaborated with UBL, driving innovation in the industry.

2008–11

Brewing More

Brewing capacity was significantly increased to 4 million cases per annum. We launched the 'Mount' brand in 2010. Total annual sales surpassed 1 million cases, reflecting strong market presence and customer trust.

2012

Launch of Lemount

The launch of the Lemount brand marked a significant milestone in the company's journey, introducing a fresh and innovative offering to the market.

2012–16

A Testament to Success

Achieving a remarkable milestone, total annual sales surpassed 4 million cases, reflecting the company's steady growth, strong market presence, and unwavering customer trust.

2017–19

State-of-the-Art Canning Line

Madhya Pradesh's first canning line was unveiled. Beer production capacity was increased to 7 million cases per annum. Distribution expanded to Uttar Pradesh and Chhattisgarh.

2020

Introduction of STOK

The introduction of STOK marked a bold step forward in the company's product offerings, quickly establishing itself as a standout in the market.

2021

Commenced Export of Beer to Dubai

The company expanded its global footprint by commencing the export of beer to Dubai, marking a significant milestone in its international growth strategy.

2022–23

Bigger Capacity, Bigger Reach

Beer production capacity increased to 10 million cases per annum, enabling greater scalability. Commenced supply in Delhi, reinforcing presence in key metropolitan markets.

2024

Strengthening Our Reach

Expanded footprint to Assam, Tripura, and West Bengal. With 20 million cases of installed capacity annually, MEBL continues to push boundaries across India.

Partnering with the best

United Breweries Limited

Since the start of commercial production, MEBL has had a strategic manufacturing tie-up with United Breweries Limited for their flagship brands.

Expansive Presence Across the Country:

We are proud to be a leading brewery in Madhya Pradesh and currently operate across 9 states in India. With plans to expand into 4 new states by FY 2024-25, we're gearing up to grow in Goa, Maharashtra, and beyond.
