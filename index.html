We are more than a beer company

At Mount Everest Breweries Limited (MEBL), we craft experiences that rise above the rest. Our journey began with a bold ambition to scale new heights in brewing. Since our inception in 1999, MEBL has been on an unrelenting climb to create beers that don't just satisfy but leave a mark.

Our Story

Brewing Greatness, One Summit at a Time

Our brewery in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, stands as a testament to our dedication, with an installed capacity of 10 lakh hectolitres (1.5 million cases per month) and one of the finest canning lines in the world. Setting up this brewery in just nine months was our first "summit" moment—proof that we're as restless as a bubbling brew when it comes to achieving greatness.

Our Story

Brewing Excellence Across India

From the heart of Madhya Pradesh to scaling new markets like Goa and Maharashtra, MEBL is on a mission to bring high-quality brews to every corner of India. Cheers to conquering peaks. A pour at a time.

Our Story

Brewed for a Better Tomorrow

Sustainability is our guiding compass as we navigate this journey. We are committed to reducing, reusing, and recycling to protect the environment, ensuring that every sip you take is as responsible as it is refreshing.

Our Story

Global Quality, Trusted Partnership

Over the years, our partnership with United Breweries has allowed us to maintain uncompromising global quality standards, making us a trusted name in the industry.
